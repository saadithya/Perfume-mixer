1. Information we collect

A. Data you create inside the app

Perfume Mixer allows you to enter and save information related to your perfume blends (for example, fragrance notes, ratios, names of blends). This data is stored locally on your device and is used only to provide the App’s core functionality.

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details (such as your name, address, or phone number) to use the App.

B. Contact information (optional)

If you contact us for support or feedback by email, we may receive information such as your email address and the contents of your message. We use this information solely to respond to you and improve the App.

C. Automatically collected information

The current version of Perfume Mixer does not intentionally collect analytics data, identifiers, or location data from within the App itself. However, your device’s operating system or the app store (for example, Google Play) may collect basic diagnostic or usage information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

2. How we use your information

We may use the limited information we handle for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the core features of Perfume Mixer.
  • To respond to your support requests, questions or feedback.
  • To improve App stability, design and user experience.

3. Data storage and security

Your blend data and recipes are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the App, this local data may be removed by the operating system.

We take reasonable steps to protect any information you share with us (for example, through support emails). However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-party services

The current version of Perfume Mixer does not integrate third-party services that collect personal information from within the App. If future versions add online features (such as cloud backup or AI-powered suggestions), this Privacy Policy will be updated to clearly describe any additional data processing.

5. Children’s privacy

Perfume Mixer is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

6. Data sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may disclose information only if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests (for example, court orders or government regulations).

7. Your choices

  • You can delete your blend data at any time from within the App (if a delete feature is provided) or by uninstalling the App, which typically removes local app data from your device.
  • You can choose whether or not to contact us by email. If you no longer want us to keep previous email communication, you may request deletion (see the Contact section below).
