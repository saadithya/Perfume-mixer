What you can do with Perfume Mixer

Whether you are a beginner or a fragrance enthusiast, Perfume Mixer makes it simple to explore new combinations and keep track of your ideas.

Create custom blends
Pick fragrance families, notes and approximate ratios to design your own perfumes.
Save your recipes
Store and organize your favorite blends so you can recreate them later.
Experiment safely
Use the app as a planning tool before mixing real oils and ingredients.
Works offline
Core features are designed to work without an internet connection.*

*If Perfume Mixer uses any online services in future versions (for example, cloud backup or AI suggestions), details will be clearly explained in the in-app privacy notice and updated in the Privacy Policy.

Privacy & data

Perfume Mixer is built with simplicity and privacy in mind. Your blends and recipes stay on your device, unless a future version offers an optional backup or sync feature that you explicitly enable.
